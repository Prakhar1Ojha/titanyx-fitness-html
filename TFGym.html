Healthy Eating Tips

  • Eat protein with every meal.
  • Drink at least 3 litres of water daily.
  • Avoid excessive processed foods.
  • Include fruits and vegetables every day.
  • Sleep for 7–9 hours.
  • Track your calorie intake.
  • Don't skip breakfast.
  • Limit sugary drinks.
  • Choose whole grains over refined grains.
  • Stay consistent rather than being perfect.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common queries about joining Titanyx Fitness.

Do I need prior gym experience?

No. Our beginner-friendly programs are designed for people with zero experience. Our certified trainers will guide you through proper form and building consistency step-by-step.

Do you provide personal trainers?

Yes. Certified personal trainers are available for all Premium and Elite members. You can also hire a personal trainer separately if you are on a Basic or Standard plan.

Can I freeze my membership?

Yes. Membership can be paused for medical or personal reasons according to our freezing policy. Please consult with the reception desk for more details.

Is parking available?

Yes. Free parking is available for all registered gym members at our facility.

Do you provide diet plans?

Yes. Every Premium and Elite member receives a personalized nutrition plan tailored to their specific weight and muscle goals.

Can I take a free trial?

Absolutely! Every new visitor gets one complimentary trial session. Fill in our contact form or visit our reception desk to register for it.
